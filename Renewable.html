Unveiling Global Renewable Energy Trends: A Data-Driven Exploration

Renewable Energy Production Insights Dashboard.

1.Introduction

In my data analysis portfolio, I explore the nuanced dynamics of global renewable energy trends. I've utilized a comprehensive dataset from Kaggle , narrowing my focus to the top 26 countries. This allows me to uncover key insights and trends in renewable energy production spanning the years from 1965 to 2022.

2.Project Overview

2.1 Objective

This project aims to shed light on both production and consumption patterns of renewable energy worldwide. The dataset includes crucial information on hydropower, wind, solar, biofuel, and more. By employing a combination of SQL and Tableau, I conducted an in-depth exploration to derive meaningful insights.

2.2 dataset

The dataset, available on Kaggle, spans from 1965 to 2022, providing a global perspective on renewable energy production and consumption.

2.3 Top 26 Countries Selection

To refine the analysis, I narrowed down the data to focus on the top 26 countries leading in renewable energy production.

3. SQL codes

3.1 Growth rate analysis for renweable energy production

I applied SQL to analyze Total Growth Rates using two methods: tracking growth from the first to the last recorded year for each country and Year-over-Year calculations.

View the SQL codes below for details.

#Total Growth Rate Analysis between first year and last year SQL:

-- Total Growth Rate Analysis between first year and last year:

WITH CTE AS (

SELECT

Entity,

Year,

SUM(Electricity_from_wind_TWh + Electricity_from_hydro_TWh + Electricity_from_solar_TWh + Other_renewables_including_bioenergy_TWh) AS TotalElectricity

FROM

dbo.prod$

GROUP BY

Entity, Year

)

SELECT

Entity,

MAX(Year) AS LastYear,

MIN(Year) AS FirstYear,

MAX(TotalElectricity) / NULLIF(MIN(TotalElectricity), 0) AS TotalGrowthRate

FROM

CTE

GROUP BY

Entity;


#Total Growth Rate Analysis; year over year (YOY) SQL:

-- Total Growth Rate Analysis; YOY

SELECT

Entity,

Year,

CASE

WHEN LAG(SUM(Electricity_from_wind_TWh

+ Electricity_from_hydro_TWh

+ Electricity_from_solar_TWh

+ Other_renewables_including_bioenergy_TWh))

OVER (PARTITION BY Entity ORDER BY Year) = 0 THEN NULL

ELSE

100* (SUM(Electricity_from_wind_TWh

+ Electricity_from_hydro_TWh

+ Electricity_from_solar_TWh

+ Other_renewables_including_bioenergy_TWh)

- LAG(SUM(Electricity_from_wind_TWh

+ Electricity_from_hydro_TWh

+ Electricity_from_solar_TWh

+ Other_renewables_including_bioenergy_TWh))

OVER (PARTITION BY Entity ORDER BY Year)) /

LAG(SUM(Electricity_from_wind_TWh

+ Electricity_from_hydro_TWh

+ Electricity_from_solar_TWh

+ Other_renewables_including_bioenergy_TWh))

OVER (PARTITION BY Entity ORDER BY Year)

END AS Yearly_Growth_Rate

FROM

dbo.prod$

GROUP BY

Entity, Year;
